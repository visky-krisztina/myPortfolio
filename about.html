about me

Hello, I am Krisztina Visky and I am a web developer. I live and work in Romania, Cluj-Napoca. I spend most of my day between kids, housework and experimenting with REACT, HTML, CSS and JavaScript, Node, Express, and interested in Python as well.

I attended a programming school for 3years, but most of all I learn by myself between changing diapers and activities with the kids. And somewhere over the years, I realized that I want to give something to the community, and started to imagine a complex website as a huge database of activities for kids, that would help mothers with activity ideas. Hence, started learning and creating different projects, like the front-end and back-end website for the church, that improve my skills to achieve my dream project.

I am not a professional, yet. I am still learning and wish to become a professional programmer.

I enjoy coding and the challenge of learning something new everyday. I am a very enthusiastic person and if there is a way to solve a problem, I usually spend my time finding it and not giving up on my ideas.
